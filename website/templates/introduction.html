Comparative Judgement Survey


Forced marriage in Nottinghamshire

This aim of this study is to rank the wards in Nottinghamshire by rate of forced marriage. In this study you will be shown pairs of wards (both city and county) in Nottinghamshire and asked to choose which ward has the higher rate of forced marriage. Your participation should take around 10 minutes.


Instructions

  1. You will be shown a pair of wards. Please click the picture of the ward which has the higher rate of forced marriage. Once you are happy with your choice, please click confirm.
  2. If your are unfamiliar with one of the wards, please click ‘I don’t know’ above the picture of the ward. You will not be asked about this ward again.
  3. If you cannot compare the pair of wards shown, please click skip on the right of the screen.
  4. We ask you do 30 comparisons. Once you have finished, click finish in the top right of the screen.
